Welcome to the UMD DIT Website

Welcome UMD DIT team to your new website! This site is designed to streamline your workflow and enhance team coordination.

The General Calendar page is your go-to resource for viewing scheduled tests. Integrated with Google Calendar, it allows for real-time scheduling and updates.

On the Documentation page, team members can download testing documents related to previously completed network tests. This section utilizes Google Drive for secure and organized document storage.

The Schedule Maintenance page is designated for setting up needed tests on network systems, ensuring that all maintenance is planned and tracked efficiently.

This Upload page is designed for providing documentation of recently completed tests. Team members can upload files directly to our Google Drive folder here.
